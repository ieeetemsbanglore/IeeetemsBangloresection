Our Story

Technology alone does not shape the future —
leadership does.

IEEE TEMS (Technology and Engineering Management Society) is a global IEEE society dedicated to engineering management, innovation strategy, and technology-driven leadership.

Through international conferences, industry engagement, peer-reviewed publications, and knowledge-sharing platforms, IEEE TEMS enables its members to transform bold ideas into scalable, real-world solutions.

Global Reach

Part of the world's largest technical professional organization with over 400,000 members across 160+ countries committed to advancing technology for humanity.

Bangalore Chapter

The IEEE TEMS Bangalore Section strengthens the global mission by nurturing a thriving ecosystem of innovators, educators, and industry leaders across India's technology capital.

Student Focus

Active student branches across Karnataka campuses — providing hands-on leadership training, mentorship, and real industry exposure to the next generation of engineering leaders.

Mission, Vision & Values
01
Our Mission

To empower engineering and technology professionals with the knowledge, skills, and networks needed to lead innovation, drive impact, and shape the future of technology management across Karnataka and beyond.

02
Our Vision

To be the premier platform in South India where technologists become leaders — bridging the gap between engineering excellence and strategic, human-centred management that creates lasting societal impact.

03
Our Values

Excellence in everything we do. Integrity in every interaction. Inclusivity across all communities. Innovation as a continuous habit. Collaboration as the engine of progress. Impact as the measure of success.

Our Journey
Foundation
IEEE TEMS Bangalore Section Established

The Bangalore Section of IEEE TEMS was formally established, bringing together technology and engineering management professionals across Karnataka under a unified platform.

Growth
Student Branch Network Expands

Established active student branches across leading Karnataka institutions including REVA University, driving grassroots-level engineering leadership development.

Industry
Industry–Academia Partnerships

Forged key collaborations between academic institutions and technology industry leaders, enabling knowledge exchange, internships, and real-world project exposure.

2026
New Leadership, Stronger Vision

Under the chairmanship of Dr. M. Devanathan, the section enters 2026 with an ambitious roadmap — more events, deeper student engagement, and a stronger industry footprint across South India.
